Major Updates

Shaping Our Future Work & Growth

This quarter brings transformative updates to our core policy landscape, designed to empower your career growth and reinforce our foundational ethical standards.

Employee Growth & IJP

  • Structured Paths: Progression is specific to your business, role type, band, and location tier.
  • Intra vs. Inter: Clear distinction between Intra-role and Inter-role promotions.
  • Growth Calculator: A new transparent tool to check your personal promotion eligibility.

Future Leader Program

  • Mobility Tracks: Structured 2-year cross-functional stints.
  • Accelerated Growth: Reduced tenure requirements for band upgrades.
  • Yearly Bonus: Fixed Award Money payout based on performance and band.

ABAC Policy

  • Zero Tolerance: Explicit ban on offering or accepting bribes or facilitation payments.
  • Clear Boundaries: Well-defined guidelines surrounding business gifts and nominal values.
  • Whistle Blower: Mandatory requirement to report any violations immediately.

IT Corner

Hyper-customized Policies for Tech & Specialized Roles

We recognize that our tech and data teams have unique operational needs. These three major updates have been specially tailored for specific IT, Analytics, InfoSec, and AI roles.

Higher Ed & Certification

  • New Category: Support for short-term professional tech certifications (e.g. AWS, CISSP).
  • Enhanced Cover: Enhanced reimbursement limits for Degrees and Tech Certifications.
  • Study Leaves: Part-time pursuers can avail additional study leave at 50% salary.

Hybrid Work Advisory

  • Tech Focused: Tailored specifically for Tech, Analytics, Data, and InfoSec units.
  • Flexibility: Entitlement of up to 4 Work from Home (WFH) days per month.
  • Accumulation: WFH days can be accumulated with a maximum 12-day quarterly cap.

Meal Allowance Benefit

  • The Benefit: A fixed monthly taxable allowance (₹2,500) for specified Tech roles.
  • Seamless Usage: Disbursed via a digital wallet/card for online food delivery or cafeteria POS.
  • Accumulation: Generous accumulation limits (up to ₹2,00,000) with full KYC.

Minor Updates

Keeping You Informed on Policy Tweaks

Policy Area Key Refinements
Recognition Policy Shifted from a "Quota" to a "Budget" system. Introduced new awards (EXCELSIOR 2.0). Revised Super Heroes reward amounts. Discontinued MSA work anniversary benefit.
BFL Certification Policy Discontinued MSA additional benefits linked to BFL certification (Prodigy/Master) upon tenure milestone to align with the R&R policy.
Promotion Policy Defined additional criteria for promotions in senior bands (GB07B to GB10A).
Distance Learning Policy Included Narsee Monjee (NMIMS) and Manipal University. Introduced Executive PGDM from IMT Ghaziabad. Revised Symbiosis and DY Patil fee structures.
Staff Welfare & Team Defined a separate User Manual for claims. Payment proof mandatory for claims over threshold limits. Clarified Central Events Management Team (CEMT) event roles.
Business Expense Policy Included specific legal expenses functionality with defined band eligibility and maximum capping limits.
Local Conveyance Policy Restructured claim limits across multiple departments. Expanded eligible modes of travel for designated FLM roles.
Company Car Policy Revised separation clause and recovery processes. Added clear guidelines for special settlement cases and stringent rules on vehicle detail tampering.
Relocation Policy Updated exploration windows. Mandated ticket booking via BFL Relocation Desk. Enhanced accommodation limits for women employees relocating to specific Tier 2/3 cities.
Two Wheeler Policy Added clarifications regarding loan types, existing loan holders, dealer interactions, and early repayment processes.
Job Rotation Policy Restructured guidelines and pool creation logic. Expanded policy coverage to include Gold Loans across all units.
Social Media Governance Removed upper limit on hashtags. Enabled interaction on posts containing Calls to Action (CTAs). Updated official logo usage guidelines.
Domestic Travel & Events Defined clear hotel accommodation occupancy rules for major business events based strictly on designated band levels.
Financial Prudence Policy Included Gold Loans. Redefined FOIR Thresholds across the organizational hierarchy to ensure continued financial safety.

HR Policy Handbook

Sometimes you don't need to read a ten-page document to find out your travel allowance limit. The BFL HR Policy Handbook is designed for quick and easy consumption.

  • Quick Answers: Find immediate answers to common questions about benefits like Flexi Pay, iCare, Mediclaim, or Local Conveyance.
  • Process Overviews: Get a high-level understanding of essential processes such as Performance Management, Job Rotations, or Employee Separation.
Note: The handbook is a compilation of policy extracts. For granular details or exceptions, always refer to the detailed policy documents.

User Guides

While policy documents explain the rules, our User Guides focus entirely on the "how," taking the guesswork out of complex system processes.

  • System Navigation: Visual screen-by-screen walkthroughs on how to raise advances and book through the BFL desk for Domestic Travel & Two Wheeler benefits.
  • Activation & Setup: Detailed instructions on downloading partner apps, completing KYC, and managing security for Meal Allowances.

Where to find them:

Easily accessible on the Policy Microsite, located right under the vernacular (regional language) policy buttons.
